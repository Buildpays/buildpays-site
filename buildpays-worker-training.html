THE GOLDEN RULES

Four things to remember

1️⃣Submit every day you work, same day. Red day on your week strip = missing timesheet = chasing you on pay day.
2️⃣Real times, always. The agreement pays you right from honest times — overtime, allowances, the lot. Edited times get flagged for a second look, so just put what happened.
3️⃣PH, RDO, approved leave — submit nothing. Those days pay themselves. If you actually worked one, tell your allocation manager.
4️⃣Something wrong? Say so early. Wrong site, wrong hours, ticket expiring — your allocation manager or the office fixes it in minutes before pay day, not after.

Paid correctly, every week. That's the whole point.
